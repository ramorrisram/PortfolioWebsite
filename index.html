A little bit about me..

Hi Im Darion Suggs From Milwaukee, WI. In my spare time, the web development community is a big part of my life. I find keeping involved helps me stay up to date. This is also my chance to give back to the community that helped me get started, a place I am proud to be part of. Besides coding I love spending time with friends and family and can often be found together going out catching the latest movie, playing games on the sofa or laughing it up on Reddit. My job involves doing what I love, Developing new websites/applications, customer relationship management (CRM) applications and developing fantastic content management systems (CMS). I spend a lot of time learning new techniques and actively help other people learn web development through a variety of help groups and writing web development tutorials for my website and blog about advancements in web design and development. Another talent other then web development i have is an Photography/Videography business. Where i record and take photos of things all around the world.
